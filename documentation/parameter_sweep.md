# Parameter Sweep Details

Dataset: SIFT1M, random queries. Sweep: Δt ∈ {0.5, 1, 2, 5, 10, 20, 50} ms × MaxBS ∈ {32, 64, 128, 256}. Arrival rate: 2000 QPS (Poisson).

---

## Batch(MV) — Time-Window Batching, MV scan

Batch size is the dominant throughput driver. With MaxBS=32, QPS saturates at ~2096–2139 regardless of Δt (AvgBS sticks at ~31.9). Larger batches yield higher QPS but at steep latency cost. Peak QPS of 2326 is achieved at Δt=50ms, MaxBS=256.

## Batch(MM) — Time-Window Batching, MM scan

Performance is highly sensitive to AvgBS. At MaxBS=32 (AvgBS≈31.9), QPS drops to 1309–1375 — below Sequential (1895) — because GEMM overhead dominates with small m. At MaxBS=256 with large Δt, AvgBS grows and Batch(MM) can outperform Batch(MV): peak QPS of 2551 at Δt=20ms, MaxBS=256 (AvgBS=178.6). The crossover where Batch(MM) begins to match Batch(MV) occurs around AvgBS≈120–130.
